A quote is an exact copy of someone else's words, usually enclosed in quotation marks and credited to the original author or speaker. Quote means to repeat the exact words of a speaker or an author. A quote is also a passage or statement repeated in this way. Quote means to cite something as a form of proof. Quote has several other senses as a verb and a noun.
A quotation is a document that a seller provides to a buyer to offer goods or services at a stated price, under specified conditions.
Also known as quotes, sales quotes or sales quotation, quotations are used to let a potential buyer know how much goods or services will cost before they commit to the purchase.
Quatation are usually not legally binding unless they're part of an official contract. However, it's generally accepted that a customer has committed to a sale, and a specific price, if they accept the quote.
