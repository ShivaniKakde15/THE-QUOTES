Success Quotes

  1. "Success is a journey, not a destination." 
                            —Arthur Ashe
  2. "Self-belief and hard work will always earn you success." 
                                           —Virat Kohil
  3. "Money won't create success, the freedom to make it will." 
                                         —Nelson Mandela
  4. "Try not to become a man of success, but a man of value." 
                                        —Albert Einstein
  5. "Behind every successful man there's a lot of unsuccessful years." 
                                                      —Bob Brown
  6. "All you need is ignorance and confidence and the success is sure." 
                                                      —Mark Twain
  7. "The dictionary is the only place where success comes before work." 
                                                       —Mark Twain
  8. "Success is the sum of small efforts — repeated day in and day out." 
                                                   —Robert Collier
  9. "Success is walking from failure to failure with no loss of enthusiasm." 
                                                 —Winston Churchill
  10. "Success is liking yourself, liking what you do, and liking how you do it."

  11. "Success is not final; failure is not fatal: It is the courage to continue that counts."

  12. "Success is a state of mind. If you want success, start thinking of yourself as a success." 
                                                                     —Joyce Brothers
  13. "If you set your goals ridiculously high and it's a failure, you will fail above everyone else's success." 
                                                                                 —James Cameron
  14. "Many of life's failures are people who did not realize how close they were to success when they gave up."

  15. "Don't be distracted by criticism. Remember — the only taste of success some people get is to take a bite out of you."

  16. "Success is blocked by concentrating on it and planning for it… Success is shy — it won't come out while you're watching." 
                                                                                                 —Tennessee Williams
  17. "The first step toward success is taken when you refuse to be a captive of the environment in which you first find yourself." 
                                                                                                         —Mark Caine
  18. "The difference between successful people and very successful people is that very successful people say 'no' to almost everything." 
                                                                                                   —Warren Buffett
  19. "Success is just a war of attrition. Sure, there's an element of talent you should probably possess. But if you just stick around long enough, eventually something is going to happen." 
                                                                                                               —Dax Sheperd
  20. "Take up one idea, make that one idea your life. Think of it, dream of it, Live on that idea let the brain, muscles, nerves, every part of your body be full of that idea, and just leave every other idea alone. This is the way to success." 
                                                                                                         —Swami Vivekananda
