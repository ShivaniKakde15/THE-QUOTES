Attitude Quotes

  1. "The only disability in life is a bad attitude."
                           —Scott Hamilton
  2. "Live life to the fullest, and focus on the positive."

  3. "Weakness of attitude becomes weakness of character."
                                       —Albert Einstein
  4. "A positive attitude is a person's passport to a better tomorrow."
                                                 —Jeff Keller
  5. "If you look the right way, you can see that the whole world is a garden."
                                             —Frances Hodgson Burnett
  6. "Positive thinking will let you do everything better than negative thinking."
                                                           —Zig Ziglar

                                                               —Hans Selye
  8. "The attitude is very important. Because, your behavior radiates how you feel."

  9. "If you don't like something, change it. If you can't change it, change your attitude."

  13. "Having a positive mental attitude is asking how something can be done rather than saying it can't be done."

                                                                                                 —William James

                                                                                                      —Mandy Hale
  17. "A positive attitude may not solve all our problems but that is the only option we have if we want to get out of problems."

  18. "Having a positive attitude isn't wishy-washy, it's a concrete and intelligent way to view problems, challenges, and obstacles."

  19. "Nothing can stop the man with the right mental attitude from achieving his goal; nothing on earth can help the man with the wrong mental attitude."
                                                                                                          —Thomas Jefferson
  20. "My attitude is that if you push me towards something that you think is a weakness, then I will turn that perceived weakness into a strength."
                                                                                                           —JMichael Jordan
